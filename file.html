Description of Side Scroller Game: The image above is a screenshot of our side scroller game. We created a game where the player can choose between 6 different sprites to play as, and their objective is to "eat" all of the food. The game scrolls through different backgrounds and various foods appear. The amount of food eaten is displayed in the top left corner of the screen, and once the player has eaten all of the food, he/she wins the game.
